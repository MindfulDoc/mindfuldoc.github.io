About Me

I am Dr. Laura Dew, a physician and mindfulness & meditation coach based in Houston, Texas. After years of witnessing the effects of stress on both patients and colleagues, I became passionate about teaching practical tools that create calm, clarity, and resilience. My coaching blends evidence-based mindfulness practices with a compassionate, accessible approach designed to support busy professionals, caregivers, and anyone seeking greater balance in their lives.

Coaching Programs

  • 1:1 Mindfulness Coaching: Personalized sessions designed to support stress reduction, clarity, and emotional balance.
  • Meditation Training: Learn practical techniques you can use daily, even with a busy schedule.
  • Workshops & Group Sessions: Tailored sessions for professionals, healthcare teams, and organizations.
